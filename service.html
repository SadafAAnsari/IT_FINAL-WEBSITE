Our Services

Capacity Building

In collaboration with relevant institutions, Influence conducts training for new and existing players along the Garments value chain.

Training and Production Centre for Cloth Industry (TPCCI)

TPCCI is a fully equipped institute specializing in diverse array of training modules for Body Wear and Garments goods production situated in Birgunj, Nepal.

Technical Services

The technical wing of Influence offers comprehensive supervisory technical support for sector projects across all phases: pre-implementation, peri-implementation, and post-implementation. These projects encompass the construction of textile manufacturing units and the development of wastewater treatment and management systems. Furthermore, the Council provides potential investors with critical information to ensure successful and productive ventures in the textile and cloth products sector.

Advisory Role

The Council advises the Government on matters concerning the processing of, and trade in, textiles and cloth products. We also provide guidance to textile Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) on adopting best practices to enhance their operations. This advice is grounded in evolving customer preferences and market trends to help them stay competitive and responsive to the demands of the cloth industry.
